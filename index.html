OverFarming - Ben Z

Over Farming or Over Cultivation conrtibutes to a massive problem in today's society. Simply put, it's the practice of using soil and farmland time and time again, until it's unusable. The practice of overfarming has gone on for many years and its results prove to be devastating.

This website aims to explain the effects of overfarming on our already torn ecosystem, and how we can fix it.

This is a project by Ben Z for high school, all sources are in the Credits section.
